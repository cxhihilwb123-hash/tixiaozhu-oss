## Maintainer Principles

- AGPL-first: the public repository remains open source under `AGPL-3.0-or-later`.
- Production-readiness-first: local demos must not be described as commercial production readiness.
- Privacy-first: screenshots, QA artifacts, logs, and examples must not include real student data, secrets, tokens, payment credentials, or private deployment details.
- Evidence-first: claims about readiness should be backed by CI, audit scripts, screenshots, or documented reproduction steps.
