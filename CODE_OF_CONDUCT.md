# Code of Conduct

Tixiaozhu welcomes thoughtful, practical, and respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Focus criticism on code, docs, product behavior, and evidence.
- Keep discussion relevant to the issue or pull request.
- Protect privacy, especially around student data, credentials, and deployment details.
- Assume good faith while still asking for clear verification.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information, credentials, or personal data.
- Deliberately misleading license, commercial-use, or security claims.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or repeated low-quality submissions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may block contributors when necessary to protect the project and community.

For commercial-use questions, see [COMMERCIAL.md](COMMERCIAL.md).
